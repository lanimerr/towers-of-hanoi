Towers of Hanoi

Instructions:

  1. Open your console (developers tool).
  2. Your beginning board has 3 pegs with 5 discs on the first peg.
  3. The goal of the game is to move around the discs on the pegs until you get all 5 discs onto another peg, in the order of 5, 4, 3, 2, 1.

The Rules

  1. Only one disc can be moved at a time.
  2. A larger disc cannot go on top of a smaller disc.

Controls

  • In the console type moveDiscs(_,_) to move around your desired discs to its spot.
    • Ex. moveDisc(1,3) will move the first disc from peg 1 to peg 3.
  • You are able to create a new board with more pegs or more discs if you more of a challemge. Use boardObj.createBoard(_,_) with your number of egs first and number of discs last.
  • boardObj.resetBoard() will reset your board back to the initial set-up.
